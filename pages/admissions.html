Requirements & Process

Eligibility Requirements

- Minimum 60% marks in Intermediate (FSc/ICS) or equivalent.

- Valid entry test score (NTS or UOG Entry Test).

Process Steps

1. Fill out the online application.

2. Submit required documents.

3. Appear for the entry test.

4. Check merit list and pay fees.
