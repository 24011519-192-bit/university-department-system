About the Department

Learn about our history and achievements.

Our Mission

To produce highly skilled computer science professionals who are equipped with the knowledge and capabilities to solve complex real-world problems.

Our Vision

To be recognized globally as a premier center of excellence in computer science education, research, and innovation.

Department History

The Department of Computer Science at the University of Gujrat was established to cater to the growing need for IT professionals. Over the years, we have grown significantly, producing top-tier graduates who are leading the tech industry both locally and internationally.

Key Achievements

Top Graduates

Consistently producing gold medalists at national tech competitions.

Research Impact

Industry Collaboration

Strong ties with software houses for student internships.

Events & Workshops

Hosting annual tech fests and multiple skill development workshops.
